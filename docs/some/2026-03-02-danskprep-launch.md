## LinkedIn Post

I'm preparing for Prøve i Dansk 3 — and I couldn't find a good free tool to practice with.

So I built one.

Introducing DanskPrep — a free, open-source web app for anyone preparing for Danish integration language exams (PD1, PD2, PD3, Studieprøven).

Here's what it does:

**292 exercises** across 7 types — fill-in-the-blank, multiple choice, word order, error correction, conjugation, and more. All mapped to real exam topics like inverted word order, noun gender, and subordinate clauses.

**Vocabulary drill** (my personal favorite) — it tests you in 5 different ways: translate both directions, fill context gaps, complete paradigm tables, and pick the right form. Way more effective than just reading word lists.

**AI Danish tutor** — a chatbot you can ask grammar questions, practice conversation with, or get explanations in plain language. Like having a patient teacher available 24/7.

**Podcast-based listening** — real Danish podcast episodes with auto-generated comprehension quizzes and vocabulary highlights. Practice listening the way native content actually sounds.

**Bubble Word Game** — a playful way to discover new Danish words. They float up the screen, you click to reveal meanings, and compete on a leaderboard. It's the first thing you see on the welcome page.

Everything runs on spaced repetition (FSRS) — the app remembers what you struggle with and reviews those words more often. It works offline too.

The whole thing is free: https://danskprep.vercel.app/welcome

**If you're preparing for a Danish exam, try it out.** It currently covers PD3 Module 2 with 277 vocabulary words and 6 grammar topics.

**If you teach Danish or have exam material to share** — contributions are very welcome. Every data source is appreciated and recognized. The project is open source on GitHub: https://github.com/YanCheng-go/danskprep

Whether you have exercises from your own courses, corrections to existing content, or ideas for new features — I'd love to hear from you. This is built by a learner, for learners.
